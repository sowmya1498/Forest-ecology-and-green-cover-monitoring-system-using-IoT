FOREST ECOLOGY AND GREEN COVER MONITORING

For a greener tomorrow


Forest

Recorded Forest Area (RFA) in Karnataka is 38,284 sqkm of which, 28,690 sqkm is reserved forest; 3,931 sqkm is protected area and 5,663 sqkm is unclassed forests.

Tiger

Karnataka has been declared as No. 1 State in Tiger Population in the country. At present the Tiger population is estimated around 300 which is 20% of the tiger population of India.

Elephant

Karnataka is now home to 6,072 asiatic elephants which is 25% of the elephant population of India
